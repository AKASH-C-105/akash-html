"Dark" is a German science fiction thriller streaming television series co-created by Baran bo Odar and Jantje Friese. The series debuted on Netflix in December 2017 and concluded with its third season in June 2020. Set in the fictional German town of Winden, the show follows multiple interconnected families as they uncover a complex web of secrets spanning several generations. The plot involves time travel, family drama, and a mysterious event that links the characters' lives across different time periods.
